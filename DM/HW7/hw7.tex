  \begin{problem}{1}
    Let $P(n) = 5^n + 9 < 6^n$. $P(2)$ is true. Suppose that $P(k)$ is also true. \\
    $P(k+1):$ $5^{k+1}+9<6^{k+1}$ \\
    $5^{k+1} + 9 < 5(5^k + 9) < 6(5^k + 9)$ \\
    $6(5^k + 9) < 6 * 6^k$ \\
    $5^k + 9 < 6^k$ is true by our assumption, that's mean that $P(k+1)$ is also true.
  \end{problem}
